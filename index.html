Our story

A sweet triumph.

Cravery began with a craving. Hunting for the perfect cassata at midnight, founder Priya Vasireddy started layering ice-creams at home. Friends insisted on paying — and a café was born. What started as a craving became two of Hyderabad's most-loved cosy corners.

Her mantra, then and now: customer satisfaction, quality, and employee happiness.

— Priya Vasireddy, founder
